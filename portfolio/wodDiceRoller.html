World Of Darkness 5th Edition Dice Roller

This is one of my first web development projects. It is a dice roller for all three of the current World of Darkness 5th Edition Table-Top Role-Playing Games created by White Wolf Publishing. The dice roller is meant for players who do not have a set of dice to play the games. While it is primarily meant for smart phones use, it can be used on tablet or desktop just as easily.

The site uses HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript for the dice logic.

Feel free to browse the screenshots or use the dice roller yourself by following the link below!
